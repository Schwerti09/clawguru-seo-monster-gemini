## Goal

- Twitter/X (DevOps, Security, Self-Hosted communities)

## Outreach Process

### Step 1: Identify Targets (30 creators)
Focus on creators with:
- 1K+ followers on Twitter/X or 500+ connections on LinkedIn
- Active engagement with DevOps/Security content
- Audience interested in self-hosted, hardening, or infrastructure security
- History of sharing valuable resources (not just self-promotion)

### Step 2: Personalize Each DM
- Reference specific content they've created
- Mention why their audience would be a good fit
- Keep it short and value-first
- No pressure or salesy language

### Step 3: Track Responses
- Use a spreadsheet to track: Creator name, platform, date sent, response status, follow-up date
- Follow up after 3-5 days if no response
- If interested, provide affiliate signup link: mailto:affiliates@clawguru.org

### Step 4: Onboard New Affiliates
- Send welcome email with affiliate dashboard access
- Provide marketing materials (banners, copy templates)
- Offer to create custom assets if needed
- Check in weekly to support and optimize

## Success Metrics
- 30 DMs sent
- 10+ responses
- 5+ new affiliate signups
- 2+ active affiliates with first referral within 30 days

## Notes
- Be authentic and genuine - creators can smell spam
- Focus on relationship building, not just transaction
- Offer value first (share their content, engage with their posts)
- Don't be discouraged by non-responses - it's a numbers game
