# Welcome to ClawGuru

You downloaded the **Critical Incident Response Toolkit**.

This kit is meant for real operational pressure:

- fast triage (first 15 minutes)
- containment and threat containment
- recovery orchestration
- evidence collection and communication templates

## How to use this toolkit

1. Start with `playbooks/15-minute-incident-triage.md`.
2. Pick the matching `runbooks/` scenario.
3. Use `docs/` templates to document your timeline and evidence.
4. Run `scripts/` to collect quick forensic signals.
