# Partnership Pitch: Hetzner & DigitalOcean Affiliate Exchange

## Goal
Establish affiliate exchange partnerships with Hetzner and DigitalOcean to drive mutual growth.

## Value Proposition for Partners

### For Hetzner/DO:
- **Qualified traffic**: ClawGuru users are actively looking to host and secure infrastructure
- **High intent audience**: Users checking security are ready to deploy
- **Complementary product**: Security hardening + hosting = complete solution
- **Revenue share**: We'll promote their hosting with our affiliate links
- **Brand alignment**: Both focus on developer-friendly, self-hosted solutions

### For ClawGuru:
- **Distribution**: Access to their customer bases (millions of developers)
- **Trust transfer**: Endorsement from trusted hosting providers
- **Revenue**: Affiliate commissions on hosting referrals
- **Market validation**: Partnership validates our product in the ecosystem

## Partnership Models

### Model 1: Simple Affiliate Exchange
- We promote Hetzner/DO with our affiliate links
- They promote ClawGuru with their affiliate links
- Both earn commission on referred customers
- No integration required
- Quick to implement

### Model 2: Co-Marketing Campaign
- Joint webinar: "Secure Your Infrastructure on Hetzner/DO"
- Co-authored blog posts
- Shared social media campaigns
- Cross-promotion in newsletters
- Deeper integration, more commitment

### Model 3: Product Integration
- ClawGuru runbooks include Hetzner/DO-specific guides
- Hetzner/DO marketplace listing for ClawGuru
- One-click deployment from their marketplace
- Deepest integration, highest impact
