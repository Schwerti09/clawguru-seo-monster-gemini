# Legendary: Critical Infrastructure Defense (High-Level)

## Goal
Blueprint for hardening safety/availability-critical environments.

## Focus areas
- Strong identity boundaries
- Segmentation by zones
- Immutable backups + recovery drills
- Change governance + emergency lanes

## Baseline controls
- Default-deny network policy
- Break-glass access with offline storage
- Configuration drift detection
- Continuous integrity monitoring

## Incident posture
- Prioritize safety and availability
- Formal comms and decision logs
