# Beta Tester Outreach - Phase B Task B1

## Goal
Recruit 50 beta testers via Discord, X (Twitter), and IndieHackers for launch testimonials.

## Target Audience
Beta testers on:
- Discord (security, DevOps, self-hosted communities)
- Twitter/X (DevOps, Security, Self-Hosted communities)
- IndieHackers (Security founders, homelab enthusiasts)

## Value Proposition for Beta Testers
- **Free Pro access** during beta period (value: €49/mo)
- **Early access** to new features before public launch
- **Influence** product direction with direct feedback
- **Recognition** - featured in launch testimonials
- **Lifetime discount** - 30% off Pro subscription after launch

## Outreach Process

### Step 1: Identify Targets (50 total)
- **Discord:** 20 users from relevant communities
- **Twitter/X:** 20 users with 1K+ followers
- **IndieHackers:** 10 security/DevOps founders

### Step 2: Personalize Each DM
- Reference specific content they've created
- Mention why their expertise is relevant
- Keep it short and authentic
- No salesy language

### Step 3: Track Responses
Use spreadsheet to track:
- Name
- Platform
- Date sent
- Response status (interested/no response/declined)
- Beta account created (yes/no)
- Testimonial received (yes/no)

### Step 4: Onboard Beta Testers
- Send beta invite link
- Provide quick start guide
- Schedule feedback session (optional)
- Request testimonial after 7 days of use

### Step 5: Collect Testimonials
Template for testimonial request:
```
Hey [Name]!

Thanks for testing ClawGuru! Quick favor: Would you be willing to provide a short testimonial for our Product Hunt launch?

2-3 sentences is perfect - just your honest experience with the platform. We'll feature it in our launch assets with attribution.

Example format:
"ClawGuru helped me [specific benefit]. The [specific feature] was especially useful for [use case]."

No pressure if you don't have time - just wanted to ask since your feedback has been valuable.

Best,
[Your Name]
```

## Success Metrics
- 50 DMs sent
- 20+ beta testers signed up
- 10+ testimonials collected
- 5+ testimonials featured in launch assets

## Notes
- Be authentic and genuine
- Focus on relationship building, not just transaction
- Offer value first (engage with their content before outreach)
- Don't be discouraged by non-responses - it's a numbers game
- Quality over quantity - 10 great testimonials > 50 mediocre ones
